Downloads

Welcome to my downloads page. Here you'll find a collection of Blender assets and characters I decided to make available for anyone to download. These were posted with the hope they would let someone learn. One of the biggest areas I struggled with when learning Blender was wondering how to put all the Blender donut tutorial knowledge together into something cohesive.

I hope this helps someone learn something!

Credit?

Because these files are provided for learning purposes, I do not wish for them to be used in a commercial or otherwise public way that would require credit. That does not mean I don't want credit, it just means the downloads provided here are not meant to be used for a commercial or otherwise public work.
